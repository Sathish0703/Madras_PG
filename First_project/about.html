About Chennai PG Connect

Connecting people with perfect PG accommodations in Chennai since 2020

Our Story

Chennai PG Connect was founded in 2020 by a group of IT professionals who experienced firsthand the challenges of finding quality PG accommodations in Chennai. After facing numerous difficulties in their own search - from misleading listings to lack of transparency - they decided to create a solution.

What started as a small platform to help friends find PGs has now grown into Chennai's most trusted PG rental platform, serving thousands of students and working professionals every month. We're proud to have helped over 10,000 people find their perfect PG home in Chennai.

Our mission is simple: to make finding and listing PG accommodations in Chennai easy, transparent, and stress-free for everyone involved.
