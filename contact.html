Frequently Asked Questions

To list your PG, simply register as a PG owner on our platform. Once registered, you can add your PG details, upload photos, set pricing and amenities. Our team will verify your listing before it goes live, usually within 24 hours.

For PG seekers, our platform is completely free to use. For PG owners, we offer both free and premium listing options. Premium listings provide more visibility and additional features for a small monthly fee.

We verify all PG listings through a multi-step process including document verification and physical checks for premium listings. Look for the "Verified" badge on PG listings, and always schedule a visit before making any payments.

If you encounter any issues with a PG listed on our platform, please contact our support team immediately through this contact form or call our customer support number. We take all complaints seriously and will work to resolve the issue promptly.

To promote your PG listing, consider upgrading to a premium listing for better visibility. You can also share your listing link on social media, ensure your photos are high quality, keep your availability updated, and promptly respond to inquiries.
